About The Church

Learn More About Deeper Life Bible Church

Deeper Life BIble Church is a church built on the true standard of the bible which help true christains to live an exemplary christain life. Deeper Life Bible Church was established in the year 1973 with few members and since then the church has being increasing at a very fast rate. The church split up into regions, zones, groups and even districts due to its alarming population. Presently, Depper Life Bible Church is now in every state in NIgeria and also in most countries all over the globe.

Deeper Life Bible Church holds their weekly services on Sunday morning titled Sunday Worship Service where members are taught to search and learn from the scripture before the final message of the day, on Monday evening titled Monday Bible Study where the founder himself Pastor W.F. kumuyi teaches expository word of God directly from the throne of grace and on Thursday titled Thursday Revival and Evangelical Trainning Service where member are taught on how to evangelise. Finally, every third thursday of the month, there is always Thursday Revival Hour in which the founder teaches on christains life revival.

Presently, the founder of the church has being conducting Global Crusades from one state to another and the crusades which has changed many lives as it is being veiwed globally. Many great miracles has emerged since the commencement of the crusade in 2020; many lame walked, many blind received the sights, cancer issues solved to mention a few as the miracles are infinite. Also, the youths of the church are not left out as the convener of the global crusade also prepare a programme for the youth titled "IMPACT". This programme grooms youths spiritually, academically and financially and has been a blessing to many youths. God has really been using this great man of God through these programmes.
