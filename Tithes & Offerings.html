Tithes, Offerings and Donation to the Church

God said in Malachi chapter 3 verse 10 "Bring ye all tithes into the storehouse, that there may be meat in mine house and prove me now herewith, saith the lord of host, if I will not open you the windows of heaven, and pour you out a blessing, that there shall not be room enough to recieve it." God commands all believers to give and promises an overflow of blessings to cheerful givers. Therefore we are encouraged to give to God. Also, we can give in kind by helping one another as this also attracts blessings from God.
